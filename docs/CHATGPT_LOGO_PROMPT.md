## START PROMPT

Design a premium circular bakery logo for a real home bakery brand:

**Brand name:** Knead To Know Sweet & Sour  
**Owner:** Wendy Mercado  
**Location:** Daniel Island, South Carolina (Lowcountry coastal)  
**Positioning:** Boutique home bakery — sourdough breads, cookies, bagels, pastries. Pre-orders only. Warm, neighborly, editorial — NOT corporate, NOT generic AI bakery clipart.

### LOGO FORMAT

- Primary mark: circular badge / seal (works at 48px favicon up to 500px hero)
- Must read clearly on white AND cream (`#F8F4ED`) backgrounds
- Export-ready: flat vector-style appearance, crisp edges, no muddy gradients
- Optional wordmark lockup below circle: "Knead To Know" (bold) + "Sweet & Sour" (lighter, coastal blue)

### VISUAL CONCEPT (pick ONE direction and execute fully)

- **Direction A — Wheat + Harbor:** Stylized wheat stalk or rolling-pin motif integrated into a circular seal; subtle wave or harbor line at bottom suggesting Daniel Island coast.
- **Direction B — Monogram:** Elegant "K" letterform inside circle with small wheat accent; feels heritage bakery meets modern boutique.
- **Direction C — Iconic loaf:** Minimal sourdough loaf silhouette as center icon, surrounded by thin circular border — restrained, not cartoonish.

### COLOR PALETTE (strict — do not invent new colors)

| Color                 | Hex                      | Use                    |
| --------------------- | ------------------------ | ---------------------- |
| Primary coastal blue  | `#3B6E91`                | Harbor Deep            |
| Secondary harbor blue | `#6A9EC0`                | Soft accents           |
| Accent wheat          | `#C2A878`                | Small details only     |
| Ink black             | `#111111`                | Borders, primary text  |
| Navy                  | `#1F3447`                | Optional depth         |
| Background            | white or cream `#F8F4ED` | NO pink, teal, or neon |

### TYPOGRAPHY FEEL (if wordmark included)

- Display: Fraunces-like serif — warm, editorial, not script
- "Knead To Know" in black, weight 700
- "Sweet & Sour" in `#3B6E91`, slightly smaller, letter-spaced

### STYLE RULES

- Thin black border (1px feel) on circle edge — matches website card system
- Boutique coastal editorial — Daniel Island farmers market sign, not supermarket chain
- **NO:** cupcakes with faces, cartoon chefs, 3D glossy renders, stock-photo realism, cake-boutique luxury vibes, heavy drop shadows, busy backgrounds
- **YES:** calm negative space, legible at small sizes, one clear focal icon

### DELIVERABLES TO DESCRIBE IN YOUR RESPONSE

1. Full-color circular logo on white
2. Same on cream `#F8F4ED`
3. Monochrome black version for single-color printing
4. Favicon-safe simplified icon (wheat or K monogram only)
5. Brief rationale: why it fits a Daniel Island pre-order home bakery

### REFERENCE MOOD (words only)

Lowcountry coastal morning, linen apron, sourdough scoring lines, neighbor dropping by for a loaf, soft harbor light.

**If generating an image:** square 1024×1024, centered circular mark with generous padding, high contrast, professional brand presentation mockup.
