# Knead To Know Website Product Card Asset Package

## What This Package Contains

This package contains 16 individual Knead To Know product card PNG assets for the bakery website. Each card is a branded product-card/container asset designed to hold a real bakery product photo inside the large empty image area.

These files are **not product photography**. They are reusable website UI assets for menu/product sections, homepage featured products, preorder flows, and category grids.

## Website Context

The Knead To Know bakery website is being built by stripping the Spilled Milk website down to its structural skeleton and rebranding it completely for Knead To Know. Spilled Milk should only be used as a layout/code starting point. All visible identity, colors, typography, product references, copy, UI treatment, metadata, alt text, and imagery must become Knead To Know-specific.

## Design System

The cards follow the Knead To Know logo’s visual language:

- Bright white card surfaces
- Transparent outside canvas
- Transparent product-photo frame area
- Coastal blue linework
- Deep navy typography
- Thin decorative borders
- Wheat and botanical motifs
- Heritage bakery styling
- Elegant serif typography
- High whitespace
- Premium boutique bakery presentation

## How Grok/Codex Should Use These Assets

Place actual bakery product photos into the large empty transparent image area of each card. The product image should be aligned inside the framed photo window without covering the card borders, typography, price, or pre-order button area.

Recommended implementation pattern:

1. Render the product photo behind or inside the card’s transparent image window.
2. Keep the PNG card overlay at its original aspect ratio.
3. Preserve the transparent outer canvas and product-image window.
4. Do not flatten the cards onto colored, textured, or gradient backgrounds.
5. Use a bright white page background and generous whitespace.
6. Keep product names, prices, categories, and CTAs aligned with the manifest.

## Important Rules

- Do not replace these with generic ecommerce cards.
- Do not crop decorative borders.
- Do not distort the card proportions.
- Do not use Shopify-template styling.
- Do not add bright colors, heavy gradients, startup UI, cartoon graphics, or rustic kraft-paper styling.
- Keep the final site bright white, coastal blue, deep navy, refined, artisan, and boutique.
