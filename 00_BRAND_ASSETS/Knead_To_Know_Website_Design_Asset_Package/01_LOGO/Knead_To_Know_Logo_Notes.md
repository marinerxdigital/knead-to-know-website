# Knead To Know Logo Notes

## Included Logo

`Knead_To_Know_Primary_Circular_Logo.png`

## Recommended Use

- Website header brand mark
- Footer brand block
- Social media profile image
- Packaging sticker
- Cookie box label
- Business card
- Invoice branding
- Signage or printed collateral

## Usage Rules

- Keep the logo on bright white, soft neutral, or transparent backgrounds.
- Preserve the circular badge shape and full border.
- Do not distort or stretch the logo.
- Do not recolor the logo outside the established blue, white, and black palette.
- Use adequate padding around the logo so the circular border does not feel cramped.
